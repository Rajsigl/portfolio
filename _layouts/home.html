Elayna Rajsigl

I was born and raised in small town Chatham-Kent in the countryside. I moved to Windsor 3 years ago with my fiancé, but I also attended University of Windsor before officially moving here. I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts and a Minor in Psychology. My art focuses on abstraction and multimedia of wet, dry materials. I love experimenting with new materials and see how they react to get different textures and dimension to my work.

After, graduating from ‘The U’ I started working for an organization called ‘Art InDeed’ where I provided art services to places like Hospice, Alzheimer’s Society, Roots 2 Wings, and many more local Windsor services. This experience then had me contemplating getting a Masters in Art Therapy so I could further help others through the process of creating. However, this practice isn’t very popular in Windsor and school is very sparse in Ontario, so I then started to consider Graphic Design. With Graphic Design I was very attracted to the fact that jobs are more available, and I would still be able to be creative. I really want to utilize my degree in Fine Arts and I think Graphic Design is a natural path to my learning. As a second year graphic design student, I really enjoy working in photoshop, illustration, and look forward to learning about HTML and CSS.
